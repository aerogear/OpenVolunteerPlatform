#Open Volunteer Platform CLI

The command-line tool (CLI) helps you bootstrap your Open Volunteer Platform template.

#Requirements

  • Node.js 10.0.0 or later version
  • Docker and Docker compose

#Usage

  1. Execute starter
npx openvp init yourproject
  1. Answer the question prompts and pick the template that suits your needs.
